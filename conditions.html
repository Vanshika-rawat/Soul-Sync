Adjustment Disorders

  • Difficulty coping with life changes or stressful events.

Alcohol-Related Disorders

  • Problematic patterns of alcohol use leading to impairment or distress.

Anxiety Disorders

  • Excessive worry, fear, or nervousness interfering with daily life.

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)

  • Inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity affecting behavior.

Autism Spectrum Disorders (ASD)

  • Challenges with social interaction and communication, along with restricted interests.

Bipolar Disorders

  • Extreme mood swings between depressive and manic episodes.

Body Dysmorphic Disorder (BDD)

  • Obsession with perceived flaws in physical appearance.

Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD)

  • Unstable moods, relationships, and self-image.

  • Persistent patterns of behavior violating societal norms and rules.

  • Persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest.

  • Difficulty controlling impulses and behaviors.

  • Disconnection from reality, memory loss, or identity disturbances.

  • Involuntary urination or defecation in inappropriate places or situations.

  • Distorted eating behaviors and body image concerns.

  • Discomfort or distress due to a mismatch between biological sex and gender identity.

Hoarding Disorder

  • Persistent difficulty discarding possessions, leading to clutter and distress.

Intellectual Developemnt Disorder

  • Intellectual and adaptive functioning deficits present from childhood.

Medical Conditions with Mental Health Symptoms

  • Physical illnesses causing psychiatric symptoms.

Medication-Induced Movement Disorders and Other Adverse Effects of Medication

  • Side effects from medications affecting movement or mental health.

Neurocognitive Disorders

  • Cognitive decline due to underlying neurological conditions.

Neurodevelopmental Disorders

  • Early brain development disorders affecting behavior and learning.

Obsessive-Compulsive and Related Disorders

  • Intrusive thoughts and repetitive behaviors causing distress.

Paraphilic Disorders

  • Atypical sexual interests causing distress or impairment.

Personality Disorders

  • Enduring patterns of behavior deviating from cultural norms.

Posttraumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

  • Anxiety disorder following exposure to traumatic events.

Schizophrenia Spectrum and Other Psychotic Disorders

  • Disturbances in thinking, perception, and behavior.

Sexual Dysfunctions

  • Difficulties during sexual response cycle stages.

Sleep-Wake Disorders

  • Disturbances in sleep patterns causing impairment.

Somatic Symptom and Related Disorders

  • Excessive concern over physical symptoms affecting functioning.

  • Difficulty with academic skills despite normal intelligence.

Substance-Related and Addictive Disorders

  • Problematic substance use causing distress or impairment.

Suicidal Behavior Disorder

  • Self-harming behaviors or suicide attempts due to distress.

Tic Disorders

  • Involuntary repetitive movements or vocalizations.

Trauma- and Stressor-Related Disorders

  • Conditions triggered by traumatic or stressful events.
