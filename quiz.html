Adjustment Disorder Self-Assessment Quiz

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed by everyday tasks or responsibilities?

2. How frequently do you experience difficulty sleeping or changes in your sleeping patterns?

3. How often do you feel irritable or on edge?

4. How frequently do you experience changes in appetite or weight?

5. How often do you feel disconnected or estranged from others?

6. How frequently do you experience feelings of sadness, hopelessness, or despair?

7. How often do you find it challenging to concentrate or make decisions?

8. How frequently do you engage in avoidant behaviors, such as avoiding social situations or responsibilities?

9. How often do you experience physical symptoms, such as headaches or stomachaches, without medical explanation?

10. How frequently do you feel overwhelmed by changes in your life or circumstances?

Alcohol-Related Disorder Self-Assessment Quiz

1. How often do you consume alcohol?

2. How many drinks do you typically consume per drinking session?

3. How often do you experience blackouts or memory lapses after drinking?

4. Have you ever felt guilty or ashamed about your drinking habits?

5. How often do you drink to cope with stress or emotional problems?

6. Have you ever experienced withdrawal symptoms when attempting to quit or cut down on drinking?

7. How often do you neglect responsibilities at work, school, or home due to drinking?

8. Have you ever been involved in risky or dangerous situations while drinking?

9. How often do you find yourself unable to stop drinking once you start?

10. Have you ever tried to cut down or control your drinking?

2. How frequently do you have trouble organizing tasks and activities?

3. How often do you avoid or delay starting tasks that require a lot of thought?

4. How frequently do you lose things necessary for tasks or activities?

5. How often do you become easily distracted by unrelated thoughts or stimuli?

6. How frequently do you forget to do routine daily activities, such as paying bills or running errands?

7. How often do you find it difficult to follow through on instructions or complete tasks in your work or personal life?

8. How frequently do you interrupt others or intrude on their conversations or activities?

9. How often do you feel restless or find it difficult to sit still in situations where it is expected?

10. How frequently do you engage in activities that are excessively risky or have a high potential for negative consequences?

Bipolar Disorder Self-Assessment Quiz

1. Have you experienced periods of unusually elevated mood or energy, often referred to as manic episodes?

2. Have you ever experienced periods of unusually low mood, known as depressive episodes?

3. How often do you experience abrupt changes in mood and energy levels?

4. Have these mood swings significantly affected your ability to function at work, school, or in relationships?

5. Have you engaged in impulsive or risky behaviors during manic episodes?

6. Do you experience changes in your sleep patterns during mood episodes?

7. Have you experienced changes in appetite or weight during depressive or manic episodes?

8. Have you ever experienced hallucinations or delusions during mood episodes?

9. How do you feel about seeking help or treatment for your mood swings?

10. How do you cope with stress and major life changes?

Body Dysmorphic Disorder Self-Assessment Quiz

1. Do you find yourself preoccupied with one or more perceived flaws or defects in your physical appearance?

2. How much time do you spend thinking about these perceived flaws each day?

3. How much distress or impairment do these perceived flaws cause in your daily life?

4. Have you ever sought reassurance about your appearance from others?

5. How often do you compare your appearance to others, especially in a negative light?

6. Do these concerns about your appearance significantly interfere with your social life or relationships?

7. Have you ever undergone cosmetic procedures or surgeries to fix perceived flaws?

8. How would you rate your overall satisfaction with your physical appearance?

9. Do you find yourself avoiding mirrors or reflective surfaces due to concerns about your appearance?

10. Have these concerns ever caused you to miss school, work, or other important activities?

Borderline Personality Disorder Self-Assessment Quiz

1. Do you have intense and unstable relationships, often marked by idealization and devaluation?

2. How often do you experience rapid mood swings, often lasting a few hours to a day?

3. Do you often engage in impulsive behaviors such as substance abuse, reckless driving, or binge eating?

4. How often do you experience chronic feelings of emptiness or boredom?

5. Have you experienced recurrent suicidal thoughts or self-harming behaviors?

6. How do you react to perceived abandonment, even if it's not real?

7. Do you experience identity disturbances or uncertainty about your self-image, goals, or career choices?

8. How often do you experience intense and inappropriate anger, often followed by shame or guilt?

9. Do you have transient, stress-related paranoid thoughts or dissociative symptoms?

10. How do you cope with emotional stress or interpersonal conflicts?

Depressive Disorder Self-Assessment Quiz

1. Over the past two weeks, how often have you felt little interest or pleasure in doing things?

2. How often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless over the past two weeks?

3. Have you had trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or sleeping too much in the past two weeks?

4. How often have you felt tired or had little energy in the past two weeks?

5. Have you experienced a significant increase or decrease in appetite or weight change in the past two weeks?

6. How often have you felt bad about yourself or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down in the past two weeks?

7. Have you had trouble concentrating on things such as reading, watching TV, or talking to others in the past two weeks?

8. How often have you had thoughts that you would be better off dead or of hurting yourself in the past two weeks?

9. Have you experienced physical symptoms such as aches, pains, headaches, or stomach problems without a clear cause in the past two weeks?

10. How often have you felt anxious, worried, or on edge in the past two weeks?

Disruptive, Impulse-Control, and Conduct Disorders Self-Assessment Quiz

1. Do you often find it difficult to control your temper?

2. Have you ever been physically aggressive towards others (e.g., hitting, pushing, or punching)?

3. Do you often break rules, defy authority, or refuse to comply with requests or directions?

4. Have you engaged in behaviors that deliberately destroy property or harm animals?

5. Do you often blame others for your mistakes or misbehavior?

6. Have you stolen from people, such as breaking into their homes or taking their possessions?

7. Do you often initiate physical fights or arguments with others?

8. Have you been expelled from school or faced legal consequences due to your behavior?

9. Do you often engage in reckless or risky activities without considering the consequences?

10. How often do you have difficulty maintaining friendships or relationships due to your behavior?

Dissociative Disorder Self-Assessment Quiz

1. Have you ever experienced memory gaps or blackouts where you cannot recall important personal information?

2. Do you often feel like you are watching yourself from outside your body, as if you're detached from your thoughts, feelings, or sensations?

3. Have you experienced identity confusion, where you feel uncertain about who you are or your sense of self?

4. Do you often feel like you're in a dream-like state or that the world around you is unreal?

5. Have you experienced sudden and unexpected changes in mood, behavior, or identity?

6. Have you ever felt like you were outside of your body during traumatic events?

7. Do you often experience amnesia, forgetting important personal information or events?

8. Have you ever felt like you were someone else or that you had different identities or personalities?

9. Do you experience significant distress or problems in your daily life as a result of these experiences?

10. Have others expressed concern or noticed changes in your behavior or memory?

Feeding and Eating Disorder Self-Assessment Quiz

1. Do you often experience intense fear of gaining weight or becoming fat?

2. How often do you engage in behaviors aimed at preventing weight gain, such as restrictive dieting or excessive exercise?

3. Do you often feel dissatisfied with your body shape or weight, regardless of actual size?

4. How frequently do you experience episodes of binge eating, characterized by consuming large amounts of food in a short period?

5. Do you often feel a lack of control over your eating behavior during binge episodes?

6. How often do you engage in compensatory behaviors to prevent weight gain after binge eating, such as self-induced vomiting or misuse of laxatives?

7. Have you experienced significant weight loss or failure to achieve expected weight gain, despite efforts to maintain or increase weight?

8. Do you often experience a distorted body image, seeing yourself as overweight even when underweight?

9. How often do you avoid eating certain foods or food groups due to fear of weight gain or perceived negative consequences?

10. Have you experienced disruptions in menstrual cycles as a result of abnormal eating behaviors?

Gender Dysphoria Self-Assessment Quiz

1. How comfortable do you feel with your assigned gender at birth?

2. Have you ever felt a strong and persistent desire to be of another gender?

3. How do you perceive yourself in terms of your gender identity?

4. Do you often experience distress related to your gender identity?

5. How do you feel about your body in relation to your gender identity?

6. Have you taken any steps to change your physical appearance to align with your gender identity?

7. How supportive are your family and friends of your gender identity?

8. Have you experienced discrimination or harassment due to your gender identity?

9. How satisfied are you with the medical and mental health support available for gender transition?

10. How hopeful are you about your future in terms of your gender identity?
